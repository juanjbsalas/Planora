Notable locations

Explore the beautiful landmarks in Andalusia

Seville's wooden umbrella.
Adalusian Wonder.

The Metropol Parasol, also known as "Las Setas de la Encarnación," is a stunning architectural marvel located in the heart of Seville, Spain. This wooden structure, designed by Jürgen Mayer, is a modern addition to the city's historic landscape, offering a unique blend of contemporary design and traditional Andalusian culture.

Setas de Sivilla, Seville's wooden umbrella is not just an architectural wonder; it has become a symbol of the city's vibrant culture and innovative spirit. Visitors can explore the structure's unique design, which features a series of large, mushroom-like canopies that provide shade and shelter in the bustling Plaza de la Encarnación.
