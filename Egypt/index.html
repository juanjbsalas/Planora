Notable locations

Explore our recommended beautiful landmarks in Egypt

Ancient Pyramids & the Sphinx.
Ancient Egyptian Engineering.

The Great Pyramid of Giza, one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, is a stunning architectural marvel located in the heart of Egypt. This pyramid, built as a tomb for the Pharaoh Khufu, showcases the incredible engineering skills of the ancient Egyptians.

The Great Sphinx of Giza, with its lion's body and human head, is another iconic symbol of ancient Egypt. Carved from a single block of limestone, the Sphinx is believed to represent the Pharaoh Khafre and serves as a guardian to the Giza Plateau.

Montaza Palace.
A Natural Beauty.

Montaza Palace is a stunning example of Islamic architecture located in Alexandria, Egypt. This beautiful palace, surrounded by lush gardens and overlooking the Mediterranean Sea, was built in the 19th century and serves as a popular tourist destination.

The palace is renowned for its stunning gardens, which feature a variety of exotic plants and flowers, as well as its beautiful views of the Mediterranean Sea. Visitors can explore the palace's rich history and architectural beauty while enjoying the serene surroundings.
